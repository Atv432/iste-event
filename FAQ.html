Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

We are a group of tech enthusiasts who want to promote innovation and creativity through our hackathon.

What is version beta?

Version beta is a testing version of our hackathon. It allows us to gather feedback and make improvements for the final version.

Who should participate?

Anyone who is interested in technology and has a passion for innovation and problem-solving is welcome to participate.

Number of members in a team?

Each team must have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.

How to participate?

You can register for the hackathon on our website and submit your team's details and project proposal.

What is selection procedure?

We will review all project proposals and select the teams with the most innovative and promising ideas to participate in the hackathon.

What are we looking for in a team?

We are looking for teams with innovative ideas, strong problem-solving skills, and a passion for technology.

Will Hackathon be conducted online?

Yes, the hackathon will be conducted online to ensure the safety and convenience of all participants.

What is the goal of the Hackathon?

The goal of the hackathon is to promote innovation and creativity in the technology industry and provide a platform for tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills and ideas.

How would I know if I got selected?

We will notify the selected teams via email and provide further instructions on how to participate in the hackathon.

Cost for participation in Hackathon?

There is no cost for participating in the hackathon. It is free and open to all eligible participants.
